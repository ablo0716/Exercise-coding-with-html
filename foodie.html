Foodie Moodie: August - November 2023

Role

Sole UI/UX Designer

Purpose

The goal of this project was to design a responsive web app for recipes, that meets the needs of its users and solves the problems they are facing with current recipe apps.

My Process

Competitive S.W.O.T Analysis, User personas, User flow diagrams Hand Drawn lofi sketches, Created mood-board, Grayscale mid fidelity, High fidelity.

Research

Competitor Overview - Kitchen Stories

Strengths

They have a beautiful and established brand that clearly shows they have the work in creating the perfect brand for recipes. They have a great SEO so their name is on the top of search results.

Weaknesses

The colors of the brand may be too overwhelming for some users.

Opportunities

Threats
